ARCHA4163: Temporal Structure
JAE HYUN LEE

NY Gallery Shows Timeline

This D3.js visualization translates a personal New York gallery list into a temporal structure. Each mark represents a show positioned by date and organized by neighborhood. The graphic keeps the same visual language as the previous spatial canvases: off-white field, black marks, and red construction lines.

Digital Object: Gallery Timeline

The timeline uses show dates from the uploaded CSV file. Shows are grouped by area, while the horizontal axis represents time.
