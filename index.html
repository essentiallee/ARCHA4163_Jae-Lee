ARCHA4163: INTERACTIVE WEB
JAE HYUN LEE

NY Gallery Visualization

This project explores New York galleries through temporal, relational, and geographic visualization.

Digital Object: Gallery Timeline

The timeline organizes gallery shows by neighborhood and date. Move across the visualization to change the selected planning date.

Digital Object: Gallery Route Network

This relational structure visualizes galleries, neighborhoods, and possible walking connections as a route-planning network.

Geospatial Visualization: Mapbox

Select two galleries to generate a walking route between them. Click a selected gallery again to remove it.
