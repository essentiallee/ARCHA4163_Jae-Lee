# Galbijjim Pixel Archive Style Guide

## Source Direction

This site borrows its design attitude from the archived AQNB homepage: dense editorial composition, sharp typographic hierarchy, black-and-white contrast, image-led blocks, and a feeling of independent web publishing. The adaptation shifts that language into a recipe archive for Korean braised beef short ribs.

All food imagery must be transformed into a 90s pixel-game style before use. No unmodified food photography should appear on the page.

## Visual Theme

- Mood: independent arts magazine meets 16-bit cooking quest.
- Layout: strong grid, blunt section breaks, compact editorial panels, no decorative floating cards.
- Texture: hard edges, visible image pixels, high-contrast borders, small interface-like labels.
- Motion: minimal. Interaction should feel like checking off a recipe quest log, not like a modern app demo.

## Image Rules

- Images should look like 1990s console game assets: visible square pixels, limited color palettes, black or deep ink outlines, and simplified shading.
- Use `image-rendering: pixelated` on all recipe images.
- Food should remain legible as food. Stylization should not hide the short ribs, radish, carrots, mushrooms, sauce, or garnish.
- Avoid photorealistic lighting, glossy stock photography, soft blur, modern vector illustration, and AI watermarks.
- Preferred framing: centered food item, three-quarter top-down view, generous margins.

## Color Palette

- Ink: `#12100e`
- Paper: `#f5ecd8`
- Bone: `#fff8e8`
- Soy brown: `#5b2d1f`
- Chili red: `#d92727`
- Pickle green: `#4b8b3b`
- Radish ivory: `#e8dcc2`
- Carrot orange: `#f28c28`
- Electric blue accent: `#2257ff`

The page should not become a single-color theme. Warm food tones are balanced with blue and green accents.

## Typography

- Primary type: system sans serif for crisp browser-native rendering.
- Display text: uppercase labels, tight hierarchy, heavy weight.
- Body text: readable, slightly compact, with generous line height.
- Letter spacing: never negative.

## Components

- Header: sticky editorial masthead with clear navigation.
- Hero: split grid with large title and pixel art image.
- Quick stats: four bordered cells, like a recipe HUD.
- Ingredient panels: compact columns with strong borders and short list items.
- Method steps: numbered quest-log rows with a small interactive done button.
- Notes: three plain editorial blocks.

## Accessibility

- Maintain high color contrast.
- Keep focus outlines visible.
- Do not rely on color alone for completed method steps.
- Images require descriptive alt text.
- All interactive controls must be keyboard reachable.

## Content Voice

- Clear and instructional.
- Slightly playful through labels such as "Build the Party" and "Cook the Quest."
- Recipe facts remain practical and grounded in the Korean Bapsang source.
